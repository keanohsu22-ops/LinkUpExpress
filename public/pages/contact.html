Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? ⌄
Browse products on the homepage, click on a product to view its details, then click "Add to Cart". When you're ready, go to your cart and click "Proceed to Payment" to complete your order.
How do I become a seller? ⌄
Create an account and then go to your Profile page. Switch your account type to "Seller". Once switched, you'll have access to the Seller Portal where you can create and manage your listings.
How is payment handled? ⌄
We accept credit and debit card payments. All transactions are processed securely. LinkUp Express does not store your card details. Sellers receive payment minus an 8% platform commission after a successful sale.
How do I track my order? ⌄
After placing an order, you will receive an order reference number on your confirmation page. You can contact the seller or our support team at linkupexpress@gmail.com with your reference number for delivery updates.
What if I receive a damaged or incorrect item? ⌄
Please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order at linkupexpress@gmail.com or 067 908 2655. Include your order reference number, a description of the issue, and photos if possible. We will assist in resolving the matter.
How do I delete my account? ⌄
You can delete your account from your Profile page under Account Settings. This action is permanent and will remove all your data from our platform. If you experience any issues, contact us at linkupexpress@gmail.com.
