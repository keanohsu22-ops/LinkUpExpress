Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

1. Introduction

LinkUp Express (Pty) Ltd ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the LinkUp Express platform, in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Full name, email address, and phone number on registration
  • Delivery address for order fulfilment
  • Payment information processed securely (we do not store card details)
  • Product listings, descriptions, and images uploaded by sellers
  • Reviews and ratings submitted by buyers

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and actions taken on the platform
  • IP address and approximate location

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process orders and facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Comply with legal obligations under South African law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure database. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (SSL/TLS) and password hashing to protect your information.

While we take every precaution, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and to notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorised access to your account.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Sellers — your name and delivery address are shared with sellers to fulfil your orders.
  • Delivery providers — courier partners may receive your address for delivery purposes.
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a South African resident, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise these rights, contact us at linkupexpress@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and to maintain your shopping cart. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect your ability to use the platform.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or a notice on the platform. Continued use of LinkUp Express after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
